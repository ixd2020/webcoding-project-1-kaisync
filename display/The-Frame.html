The Frame, 1938


Media: Oil, Aluminium, Glass

Location:Georges Pompidou Center, Paris, France

A self-portrait by Frida Kahlo, appearing to be an exploration of mixed medium. In 1939, Frida traveled to Paris to participate in "Mexique", an exhibition which featured examples of Mexican painting, sculpture, photography and popular art. Included in the exhibit was this painting. Although the exhibition was not a financial success, this painting became the first work by a 20th century Mexican artist to be purchased by the Louvre.
